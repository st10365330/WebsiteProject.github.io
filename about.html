About Us

EcoWarriors is a non-profit organization founded in 2023 with a mission to protect and preserve the environment for future generations. Our passion for environmental conservation drives our commitment to create a sustainable future.

Founders

EcoWarriors was established by Numaan Bahadur, an environmental enthusiast who saw the urgent need to address pressing environmental challenges.

Vision and Mission

Our vision is a world where every individual takes responsibility for their impact on the planet, and our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower people to become eco-warriors in their communities.

Achievements

Since our inception, EcoWarriors has successfully organized tree-planting campaigns, beach cleanups, and educational workshops on sustainable practices. Our efforts have resulted in the restoration of several acres of forest and the removal of tons of plastic waste from beaches.
